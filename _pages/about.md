Weiyu Guo is a  Ph.D. candidate from the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) now. His current research interests include temporal data mining, neural interface, spike neural network, and their applications on human machine interface, intelligent prosthesis, and metaverse. In the past three years, he has filed  15 China/U.S. patents and published  13 research papers at prestigious journals and conferences, such as ECCV, THMS, TNSRE, JNE, and IEEE RAL.
